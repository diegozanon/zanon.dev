Privacy

This website respects your privacy. Your personal data is not collected without your consent, there are no cookies or third-party analytics tools.

Newsletter: if you want, you can provide your e-mail to receive monthly updates. Your e-mail will be saved encrypted in an AWS DynamoDB database.

What is saved on your browser's local storage: your theme (dark/light) preference and posts that you have voted like/deslike.

What kind of info I collect: when you visit/click/browse the pages, I collect that information, but there is no ID mapped to you neither I save your IP address or user-agent.
